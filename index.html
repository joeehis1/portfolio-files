Who I Am

I am a self taught web developer with a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies. My passion and determination are my main drivers for learning and creation of amazing things. The journey started for me officially in March 2022, although I did play around with dreamweaver, flash and actionscript when these tools were still popular a long time ago.

The journey so far

  • Crafting web magic

    My journey started with HTML, where I discovered the building blocks of every web page. I quickly moved on to CSS and learned to leverage SCSS/SASS to bring my designs to life, make websites visually appealing and stylish, and keep my code base manageable. My constantly evolving knowledge of Javascript, React and tools in the react ecosystem enables me to add interactive and dynamic features to engage users.

  • Back-end Adventures

    My skills extend beyond just front-end! I know how to use express.js to set up fully functional web servers and I'm exploring other parts of the node.js ecosystem. Understanding server-side logic and APIs has opened up a world of possibilities for me and I hope to dive deeper as time goes by.

  • The Joy of Full Stack

    I'm fascinated by the seamless connection between the front and back end in full stack development. Integrating everything i've learned into complete web applications is my current adventure. From simple user interfaces to complex interactions, I'm eager to experiment and create.

  • Always Learning

    Learning is at the heart of my journey. From refreshing my knowldge of foundational concepts to exploring new libraries and tools, I am always excited to keep myself up to date with the ever evolving landscape.

  • Problem-solving Enthusiat

    Challenges are opportunities for growth, I embrace each coding challenge as a chance to learn and improve. Debugging and finding solutions is tough, but the sense of accomplishment is worth it every time
